Lifestyle


Habitat


Iberian Lynx live only in the woodlands and thickets of the Iberian penninsula. They generally only live in altitudes between 400 meters and 1,300 meters. These lynx like to have scrub and brush for protection and shelter, but also open pastures for hunting. With the recent fragmentation of their habitats, however, many Lynx live in more mountainous areas.

Behavior


Iberian lynx live solitary lives, only coming together to mate. Young lynx can roam around but once borders are set between two territories, they tend to be consistantly kept. They often set their boundaries

Food


The main compoenant of an Iberian Lynx's deit is the European Rabbit. This is on of the reasons that the speicies is so endangered. The already dwindling population of rabbits is suceptible to epidemics such as myxomitosis, making their population recovery slow. The Iberian Lynx population follows suit.
